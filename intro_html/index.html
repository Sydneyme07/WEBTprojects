Why is 24 Hours Enough?

Have you ever been burnt out? In need of a reset, but work is busy and you dont have enough PTO for a full vacation? Or maybe your bank account is silently protesting. Travel doesn't always mean a long getaway. Sometimes, 24 hours is all you need. A single day away can provide a much needed recharge without overplanning or breaking the bank.

How to Take a Daycation

Planning is the first step to a successful day. Start by choosing a destination. Think short flights, train rides, or drives under 2 hours long. Bring only the necessities and use time saving options such as TSA Precheck to make your travel more efficient. Keeping the logistics simple allows you to enjoy the experience rather than stressing over the details. Now that the basics are taken care of, focus on being present and making the most of your day.

Carpe Diem: Intentional Travel

Intentional travel means choosing experiences and sights that are meaningful to you rather than trying to see everything at once. Focus on a few main highlights and remember to enjoy the moment. Stop at that cute cafe on the way to your destination. Ask your Uber driver for the best Italian restaurant in town. What matters most is making your time meaningful.
